For start you need to choose one of the Capitals, Fruits, or Numbers categories. The computer will choose one random word or number from the chosen category and will display the random word as a hidden underlined word or number. You have 7 lives and you need to choose letters or numbers one by one, so if you select a wrong letter or number, you will lose one of your lives and you will push the hangman one step further to death. In the seventh wrong attempt, you will lose the game, the hangman will die, and you can play again. Just do it and enjoy the game...
